About metarecommendr

What's this about?

metarecommendr helps you find your next video game, TV show or movie by using advanced machine learning models (Natural Language Processing- Doc2Vec, Matrix (SVD), Neural Networks) to predict which media product you will like the most based on your input. It was conceived and built as part of our capstone project at New York City Data Science Academy.
